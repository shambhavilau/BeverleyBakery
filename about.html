Beverly’s has been creating custom cakes and desserts long before it was the trendy thing to do.

We started as a family bakeshop. Local customers fell in love. Word got out. Fast forward two generations and our artistry and quality continue to set the trend. Our experience is admired though not easily replicated.

Over the years our business has grown to include a talented team of professional bakers, decorators and customer service ambassadors. Even still, at the heart of it all we are a family bakery dedicated to bringing you sweet memories





Meet Jennifer

Baking, decorating, creating – these are more than what Jennifer does, they are who she is.

She grew up in a large, fun loving, bakery owning, Italian family. Many hours were spent in the kitchen, and there were parties and celebrations for every occasion. After an established career in marketing, Jennifer realized it was time to leave a job she liked and return to the life she loves.

Jen is a girly girl. She is crazy about pink, glitter, silk fabric, polka-dots, vintage china, family photographs, antique furniture, craft projects, old paintings, traveling, making memories, playing hostess, planning parties and cake designing (of course)!

When not in the bakery you can find her with her husband and four children. She is one very blessed and busy mama!
