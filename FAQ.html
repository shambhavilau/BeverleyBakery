FAQ

Do you offer consultation appointments?
Yes, we offer consultations for all weddings with 60 or more guests. During the appointment you’ll meet with one of our wedding cake consultants to discuss style and design, sizing options, flavor combinations and pricing. Plus, you’ll get to sample cake!
How do I schedule a consultation?
We schedule all appointments over the phone. When you schedule, a $25 appointment deposit is due. This deposit can be applied to your final order. If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.
How are your wedding cakes priced?
Wedding cakes are priced per serving. The per serving price is based on several factors including complexity of the cake design, additional decorative elements (such as fondant work, hand-made sugar flowers, etc.) and flavor selections.
Do you deliver?
We offer delivery with advance scheduling.Delivery rates are based on mileage from bakery to the event location.
How do I apply for job?
If you think you’d be a great addition to our team, please fill out ourEmployment Inquiry Form. We review all inquiries and will contact you to discuss next steps if there is an opening available that fits your skill set. Applications via phone, email or walk-in to the bakery will not be considered. Submit an Application.
